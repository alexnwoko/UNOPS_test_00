Predicts whether a short text describes a real disaster (1) or not (0).

The model is a TF-IDF + logistic-regression classifier (probability calibrated). It reflects patterns in ~7,600 labeled tweets and can be wrong on sarcasm, metaphor ("on fire"), or unfamiliar events. Treat the score as a signal, not ground truth.
